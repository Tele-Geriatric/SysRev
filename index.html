üî¨ Research Plan Overview

Comprehensive research plan for investigating the relationship between sleep patterns and mild cognitive impairment

1

Fundamental Relationship Analysis

Analyze the fundamental relationship between sleep patterns and mild cognitive impairment (MCI) to provide both a concise overview and comprehensive explanation of established links.

2

Scientific Literature Investigation

Investigate current scientific literature to identify specific mechanisms, pathways, and contributing factors that link sleep disturbances to MCI development or progression.

3

Research Gap Evaluation

Evaluate existing systematic reviews and meta-analyses to identify at least ten distinct research gaps suitable for International Medical Graduate (IMG) exploration.

4

Systematic Review Questions

Formulate at least ten distinct and answerable systematic review questions related to sleep and MCI based on identified research gaps.

5

Database Search Strategies

Develop comprehensive search strategies for PubMed and Embase databases, including relevant MeSH/Emtree terms, keywords, and Boolean operators.

6

Data Extraction Templates

Design detailed data extraction templates including author, year, sample size, methods, population, sleep measures, cognitive outcomes, and risk of bias assessment.

7

Risk of Bias Assessment

Create risk of bias summary table templates including standard domains (selection, performance, detection, attrition, and reporting bias) with mock examples.

8

Comprehensive Documentation

Synthesize all information into a structured document including background, research opportunities, search strategies, data extraction tables, and risk of bias assessments.
